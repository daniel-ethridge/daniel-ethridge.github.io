Audio: Of or relating to recorded, transmitted, or reproduced sound.

Vascular: Of, characterized by, or containing cells or vessels that carry or circulate fluids, such as blood, lymph, or sap, through the body of an animal or plant.

We may never fully understand why music makes us feel, but it's an interesting question to ponder. My work aims to explore this question, seeking answers at the intersection of physiology and music.
Audiovascular fits this sentiment perfectly. We can begin measure the effect that music has on us by looking at two of our organs: The heart and the skin.

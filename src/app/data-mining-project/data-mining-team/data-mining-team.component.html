Daniel Ethridge

Originally from Atlanta, Georgia, Daniel is a multi-faceted musician, sound engineer, and software engineer whose interests lie at the intersection of music, physiology, and technology. As a second year PhD student, he is researching music perception and cognition through the lens of cardiac signals such as heart rate and heart rate variability. He wants to employ methods from data mining and machine learning to take advantage of and analyze the vast and preexisting mixed audiovisual and physiological data available on the internet. Daniel received a Bachelor's degree in mathematics and music from Brevard College in Brevard, North Carolina and later a Master of Science in Music Technology from the Georgia Institute of Technology in Atlanta, Georgia. He is currently advised by Dr. Grace Leslie, professor of music and director of the Brain Music Lab at the ATLAS Institute.
